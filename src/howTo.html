This is the Live Video feed. Here you can see what the COCO-DYNAMICS-bot sees and explore the surroundings.

In the chat you can communicate with everyone else who is using the COCO-DYNAMICS-bot.

The control pad moves the COCO-DYNAMICS-bot and is therefore our means of interaction with the robot.

There are 4 directions in which you can move. Front, back, left and right, where front and back allow the robot to move forward/backward. Left and right, however, turn the robot on the spot.

Next to each arrow is a number. This indicates how many people have voted for the respective direction. Every 5 seconds the command with the most voices is executed. In case of a tie no command is executed.

The counter in the middle indicates how much time you have until the voting phase is over and the next one begins.
